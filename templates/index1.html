How It Works:

The Automatic Question Paper Generating System with Bloom's Level Indication operates by leveraging Flask-based GUI technology to provide educators with an intuitive platform. Users input the topic, desired difficulty level, and the number of questions needed. The system then dynamically generates diverse questions through a predefined pool and assigns Bloom's Taxonomy levels based on the specified difficulty. The generated questions are displayed alongside the corresponding Bloom's level, offering educators a quick and efficient tool to create well-rounded assessments. This streamlined process aims to enhance the quality of educational assessments, promote critical thinking, and ultimately contribute to a more effective and engaging learning experience for students.
